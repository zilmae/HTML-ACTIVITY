Why did I create this storybook?
I created this storybook because I wanted to share Filipino folklore in a way that is exciting and easy to understand. Writing about the Tiyanak allowed me to explore a mysterious and thrilling story while also showing the lessons it teaches, like being cautious and listening to the advice of elders. Through this story, I could use my imagination to bring the legend to life and make it more real for readers. I also saw it as a way to improve my storytelling and writing skills, turning a traditional tale into something that could entertain and teach at the same time.
