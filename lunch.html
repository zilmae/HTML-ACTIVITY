Sinigang

Ingredients: Pork, Tamarind mix, Tomatoes, Onion, Radish, Kangkong, Sitaw, Salt, Pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil pork until tender
  2. Add tomatoes and onion
  3. Pour in tamarind mix and stir
  4. Add radish and sitaw, cook until soft
  5. Add kangkong last
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Serve hot
