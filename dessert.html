Mango Sago

Ingredients: Mango, Sago pearls, Condensed milk, Evaporated milk, Cream, Sugar

Steps:

  1. Cook sago pearls until translucent, then rinse and drain
  2. Dice fresh mangoes
  3. Mix condensed milk, evaporated milk, and cream in a bowl
  4. Add sago and mango pieces
  5. Chill in the refrigerator
  6. Serve cold
