Improving Waste Management in Our Community

Waste management remains one of the most important challenges in many local communities. In Barangay Mayamot, improper disposal of garbage sometimes leads to clogged canals and polluted streets. This situation can cause flooding and health risks for residents. Everyone in the community shares responsibility in keeping the environment clean.

Local government units should continue strengthening waste management programs. Providing more garbage bins, encouraging recycling, and organizing clean-up drives can make a big difference. Schools should also educate students about environmental responsibility. Community awareness is key to solving this problem.

Residents must also take action by properly segregating waste and participating in community activities. If everyone works together, our barangay can become cleaner and healthier. Let us protect our environment today so future generations can enjoy a better community.
